# because they appear in bilingual subtitle dialog (English loanwords,
# character names, scene descriptions, etc.) and are not valid Spanish.

# Format: one lowercase word per line, # for comments, blank lines ignored
# Words with internal apostrophes (e.g., don't) are allowed.
#
# IMPORTANT: Do NOT include words that are valid Spanish:
#   - a (Spanish preposition)
#   - no (Spanish negation)
#   - he (archaic Spanish "I have")
#   - has (Spanish "you have")
#   - do (archaic Spanish "where")
#   - red, ve, sé, fui, vio (Spanish words that happen to match English)
